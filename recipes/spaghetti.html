Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish consisting of long, thin noodles served with a savory tomato-based sauce and often topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet, cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot.
  6. Pour the sauce over the spaghetti and toss until evenly coated.
  7. Divide the spaghetti among serving plates. Garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
